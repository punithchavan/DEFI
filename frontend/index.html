Welcome to Mini DeFi

Getting Started

Mini DeFi is a multi-asset lending platform supporting thousands of assets. You can deposit assets to earn interest, borrow against your collateral, and manage your portfolio with advanced batch operations.

1

Connect Your Wallet

Click "Connect Wallet" and approve the connection in MetaMask.

2

Browse Assets

Use the search and filters to find assets. Click assets to add them to your selection basket.

3

Set Proportions

In the Operation Panel, set the percentage allocation for each selected asset.

4

Execute Operations

Choose an operation (Deposit, Withdraw, Borrow, Repay, Liquidate) and execute with your proportions.

Key Concepts

  • Health Factor: Ratio of collateral to debt. Below 1.0 means liquidation risk.
  • Collateral Factor: Max % of deposit value you can borrow against.
  • Batch Operations: Execute operations across multiple assets at once.
  • Proportions: Distribute your operation amount across selected assets.
